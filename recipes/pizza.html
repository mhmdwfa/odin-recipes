Pizza

A delicious flatbread topped with tomato sauce, melted cheese, and a variety of toppings like pepperoni, vegetables, or herbs, then baked to perfection.

The ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings (e.g., pepperoni, vegetables)
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F)
  2. Roll out pizza dough and spread tomato sauce.
  3. Add shredded mozzarella, toppings (pepperoni, veggies, etc.).
  4. Bake for 10-15 minutes until crust is golden.
