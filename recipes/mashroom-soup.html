Mushroom soup

A creamy and comforting soup made with sautéed mushrooms, onions, garlic, and vegetable broth, blended to a smooth consistency, perfect for a cozy meal.

The ingredients

  • Mushrooms
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Vegetable broth
  • Cream (optional)
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Sauté 2 cups chopped mushrooms, 1 onion, and 2 garlic cloves in butter.
  2. Add 2 cups of broth and simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Blend until smooth, then mix in 1/2 cup cream.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, serve warm.
