Rice

Simple and fluffy, this versatile side dish is made by cooking rice in water or broth, often seasoned with herbs, and can accompany a wide variety of dishes.

The ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt (a pinch)
  • 1 tsp oil or butter
  • Optional: spices (like cardamom, bay leaf, or cinnamon for flavor)
  • Optional: fried onions, peas, or nuts for garnish

Steps

  1. Rinse 1 cup of rice until water is clear.
  2. Boil 2 cups of water with a pinch of salt and a little oil/butter
  3. Add rice, cover, and simmer on low heat for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Turn off heat, let it sit for 5 minutes, fluff with a fork.
